The Ascent

A date-by-date climb from fundamentals to a big-tech offer. One problem on weekdays, a push on weekends, a review every Sunday. Check things off — your progress saves automatically.

How to actually use this

The 25-minute rule

Attempt every problem solo for ~25 min. Stuck? Watch the NeetCode video, then re-code it yourself from memory — don't copy.

Pattern notes > solutions

Keep one doc. For each problem write the pattern, the trigger ("sorted array → two pointers"), and the time/space. Review it, not the code.

Spaced repetition wins

Sunday reviews re-solve your hardest problems from memory. If you fail one, flag it ⚑ and it lands in the polish phase.

Talk out loud

From week 1, narrate your thinking as if an interviewer is listening. Communication is graded as much as correctness.

Easy/Medium first

On a 30-min weekday, a Hard problem = watch the video and understand the idea. Re-attempt it on the weekend with real time.

Beyond the 150

The last 2 weeks add mock interviews, behavioral STAR stories, and a system-design primer — what actually separates offers from rejections.
