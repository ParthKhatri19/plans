Groundwork

Small, high-impact habits — move, hydrate, sleep, eat well — introduced one at a time from basics to a strong finish to the year. Check things off; your progress saves automatically.

How to make this stick

Never miss twice

Miss a day? Fine. Just don't miss two in a row. Consistency beats perfection — the streak is the strategy.

Stack your habits

Attach each new habit to one you already do: water with coffee, stretch after brushing teeth, walk after lunch.

Make it stupid-easy

15 honest minutes counts. Showing up beats the perfect workout you skip. On a busy day, do the minimum and tick the box.

Protein + plants

Build meals around a protein and a vegetable; the rest sorts itself out. Hydrate before you snack.

Sleep is the multiplier

Same bedtime, dark room, no screens 30 min before. It powers your energy, hunger, and workouts.

Progress, then intensity

First make it automatic. Then slowly add reps, weight, or minutes — week over week, not day one.
